Our Services

Choose the service that fits your needs

Car Rental

Rent a car for your journey. Choose from our fleet of well-maintained vehicles including sedans, SUVs, and electric cars. Perfect for business trips, family vacations, or when you need reliable transportation.

  • Wide variety of vehicles
  • Flexible rental periods
  • 24/7 customer support
  • Insurance included

Ride Sharing

Share rides with others going in the same direction. Reduce costs, make new friends, and help the environment by carpooling. Connect with drivers or offer your own vehicle for shared journeys.

  • Split travel costs
  • Meet new people
  • Reduce carbon footprint
  • Verified drivers & passengers

Bus Reservation

Book seats on buses for intercity and intracity travel. Reserve your spot in advance, choose your preferred seat, and enjoy comfortable, affordable transportation to your destination.

  • Advance seat booking
  • Multiple routes available
  • Comfortable seating
  • Real-time tracking

Mobile Wash x Car Detailing

Professional car washing and detailing services that come to you. From basic washes to premium detailing packages, we bring the service to your location for maximum convenience.

  • Mobile service
  • Eco-friendly products
  • Professional detailing
  • Flexible scheduling

Together for a Greener, Kinder World

GreenShare is more than a mobility platform—it's a movement. We believe in sharing, helping others, and protecting the planet. Every time you share a ride or choose an eco-friendly car, you’re making a positive impact on your community and the environment.

Our mission is to connect people, reduce emissions, and make cities across Africa and Europe greener and more caring. Join us and be part of the change!
